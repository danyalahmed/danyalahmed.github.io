Chanda Calculator Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 2, 2025

Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how the Chanda Calculator application ("App") handles information when you use it. This App is designed to help members calculate their estimated monthly Chanda contributions based on their status and, optionally, their income.

Your privacy is important to us. This App is designed with privacy as a priority.

Information We Process

To perform the calculation, the App requires you to input certain information:

  • Member Status: You need to select your status (e.g., Atfal, Student, Other). This determines the calculation rules applied.
  • Yearly Income (£): You have the option to enter your yearly income in British Pounds. This is used to calculate income-based Chanda percentages for 'Student' and 'Other' statuses. If you do not provide an income:
    • For 'Student' status, a default income (£100) is used for calculation purposes, or minimums are applied as per the rules.
    • For 'Other' status, minimum Chanda amounts are applied.

This information is used solely for the purpose of calculating the estimated Chanda amounts (Qiadat, Ijtema, Aam, Jalsa Salana) within the App according to predefined rules.

How We Use Your Information

The information you provide (Status and optional Income) is used exclusively to perform the Chanda calculation requested by you. The calculations happen locally on your device.

Data Storage and Retention

The Chanda Calculator App does not store the information you enter (Status, Income) persistently on your device after you close the App. The data is processed in real-time for the calculation and is discarded when the App session ends or the inputs are cleared.

We do not have access to the data you enter.

Data Sharing

We do not collect, store remotely, transmit, or share any personal or financial information you enter into the Chanda Calculator App with us or any third parties. All processing occurs locally on your device.

Data Security

As the App processes data locally and does not store or transmit it, the security of the data while using the App relies on the general security of your own device (e.g., device passcode, operating system security).

Children's Privacy

While the App includes an "Atfal" status for calculation purposes, the App is not intended for direct use by children without the supervision of a parent or guardian. We do not knowingly collect any personally identifiable information. The status selection and optional income are used only for the calculation as described.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy within the App or associated documentation. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
